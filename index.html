Intro

I'm an urban planning doctoral student at University of California, Los Angeles' Luskin School of Public Affairs. My research interests include affordable housing, neighborhoods and poverty, transportation, urban economics, and international development.

I previously worked as a Junior Research Fellow (Research Scholar) at New York University (NYU)'s Furman Center for Real Estate & Urban Policy. My research projects include topics in affordable housing, urban density, gentrification, the built environment, and transportation policy.

I hold a Master's degree in urban planning from NYU's Robert F. Wagner Graduate School of Public Service and a Bachelor's degree in economics from University of California, Berkeley.
